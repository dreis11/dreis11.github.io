About the Artist

Hi I'm Dale Reis. I began airbrushing motorcycles and helmets at age 16. Murals on vans were popular in the 1970's and I painted my share. It was a great creative side job while I was in high school. Through the years requests for artwork increased and I expanded my repertoire beyond automotive art.

I have airbrushed wine labels, signs, fine art, shirts, posters, wall murals; the possibilities are endless! Enjoy! I'll work with you to achieve your desired outcome.

Sincerely,
Dale Reis
