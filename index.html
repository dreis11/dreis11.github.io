Who Am I?

Hi I'm Dale Reis. I began airbrushing motorcycles and helmets at age 16. Murals on vans were popular in the 1970's and I painted my share. It was a great creative side job while I was in high school. Through the years request for artwork increased and I expanded my repertoire beyond automotive art.

I airbrushed wine labels, signs, fine art, shirts, posters, wall murals; the possibilities are endless! I'm beginning to advertise now and present to you, my varied portfolio on CD and on the World Wide Web. Enjoy! I'll work with you to achieve your desired outcome. Sincerely, Dale Reis
